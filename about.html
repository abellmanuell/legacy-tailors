About Us

Legacy Tailor Number #1 Tailoring Service

Legacy Tailors is a world class tailoring servicing company, specialized in making wears of African designs which is suitable and comfortable, especially those in the diaspora.

Our clothing designs are very creative and fit to the environmental conditions of our clients We take time to ensure that we advise our teeming clients on their choices of fabrics and designs that would suit their personality, while also ensuring that the designs we churn out continues to lead in the trend of African fashion and styles.

It is very important to note that, with the recent rise and universal acceptance of African entertainment, especially, the ones from Nigeria, there is also a sharp observation in the acceptance of fashion from Nigeria. It could be noticed in the fashion sense of some of the world top celebrities who rather chose designers and fashion style of Africa. It is in response to this growing demand that the Legacy Tailors was founded as complete home of fashion for African, especially Nigerian, clothing line.
