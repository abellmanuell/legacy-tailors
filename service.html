Our Services

Design

We evolve our own designs, but importantly staying truth to the root of our inspiration, which is Africa. We are also very radical in our design approach; this is why we consider our fashion house as nihilistic in nature, or naughty by nature if you will. Sewing: we also sew, using very modern equipment, with a team of very fashionable individual to make cloths across gender and class.

Styling

In the fashion world, “style” is usually shorthand for “personal style,” or the way an individual expresses themselves through aesthetic choices such as their clothing, accessories, hairstyle, and the way they put an outfit together. At Legacy Tailoring, we give our clients the opportunity to expresses him or herself by guiding their sense of fashion.

Stocking and Sales

Legacy Tailors also stock wears that are ready to wear. Therefore, it is part of our bragging right to dare say that we area one stop shop for African wear.

Fabric Selection Research

We understand the universally accepted criteria for fabric selection which are color, style, and client satisfaction, however above all suitability, cleanability, and longevity of fabric are what top designers don’t take for granted.
